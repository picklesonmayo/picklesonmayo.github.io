a little bit about me



My name is Nico. My journey starts as an imaginative kid, and this is my story.

I began my artistic endeavor ever since I was young, where my inspiration was solely from videogames and cartoons. My first big step was in the summer of 2016, where I had an idea of starting a YouTube Channel for people to watch. Since there wasn't anything to lose nor anything to do, I thought that would be the perfect opportunity for me, and so it was. Despite having no experience whatsoever, I launched myself into the world of YouTube. During those long weeks of summer, I loved creating and story-telling in a new form of art, especially when going blind.

Now, in 2019 and continuing, I'm still making art and becoming a part of the artist community. My most notable works are posted on my Instagram, @picklesonmayo, and on my YouTube channel. I've branched out into new forms of entertainment while still sticking to my roots and original creative aspiration. My hopes are to continue this moving to the future, make what I love a career, and most importantly to enjoy what I do forever.
